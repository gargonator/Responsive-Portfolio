About Me

Hello! Welcome to my page. I am a product strategist working and living in San Francisco, California. Currently, I work on creating new products for Salesforce Einstein, the AI division of Salesforce, with the goal of simplifying the day-to-day jobs of the business professionals that use our products. I'm passionate about how AI is changing the world of work and I am honored to have a role where I can help make it happen.

Before Salesforce, I was a strategy consultant with Keystone Strategy, advising multiple F500 technology companies on product, marketing, M&A, and operations challenges. I built my foundation in business and technology through degrees in engineering from Stanford University and Cornell University.

In my spare time, some things I enjoy are vinyasa yoga, listening to live jazz, snowboarding, and going on entry-level mountaineering adventures.
